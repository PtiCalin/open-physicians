- Region: Asia
- Country: South Korea
- Coverage: Partial; professional association register

## Access
- URL: https://www.kma.org/
- Access method: Member search portal requiring form POST and possible CAPTCHA
- Recommended rate limit: 1 request every 3 seconds; manual verification required
